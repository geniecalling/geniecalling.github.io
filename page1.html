WEDDING PLANING & DESIGNING

Weddings are magical, emotional, life-affirming events.A professional wedding planner is your ally, advocate and guide – helping you to navigate the waters and land safely on the shores of your “happily ever after”. Here comes our role!!

As wedding planning professionals, we can do as little or as much as you desire – the choice is completely up to you. Whether you need an impartial party to help with a particularly vexing decision or someone to work with you from engagement to your honeymoon departure, it is our pleasure to assist you with the planning and smooth management of your special day!

We realize that each wedding celebration is different and unique , therefore custom services can be designed to fit your particular needs. An initial wedding consultation is the first step towards “blueprinting” the details that will create the foundation of your day.
We truly believe that a wedding – no matter it’s size, complexity or budget, can benefit from the sage advice and knowledge of a professional who understands the unique configurations and architecture of this event. Please feel free to contact us so we can begin the journey towards a most memorable occasion for you and those that will celebrate your happiness!
